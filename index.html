About ECS

What is the Exhaust Control System?

A smart, AI-driven solution that transforms how commercial kitchens manage ventilation — automatically, efficiently, and intelligently.

System Definition

The AICOOKS Exhaust Control System (ECS) utilizes intelligent AI algorithms and high-precision sensors to automatically adjust damper angles and fan speeds based on real-time sensor feedback — similar to how a variable-frequency air conditioner works, but for your entire kitchen exhaust system.

Core Philosophy: "Let the kitchen breathe automatically — save energy, reduce noise, and create a comfortable cooking environment."

AI-Powered Intelligence

Smart algorithms process sensor data in real-time to optimize airflow dynamically.

Up to 80% Energy Saving

Only consume power when needed. Dramatically reduce electricity bills.

Noise Elimination

Advanced AI eliminates duct resonance and electromagnetic noise.

Real-Time Monitoring

Monitor all kitchen zones with data tracing, fault history, and remote management.

Core Components

Four Key Components Working in Harmony

01

AICOOKS Control Cabinet

How It Works

Intelligent, Automated, Seamless

Our system continuously monitors your kitchen and automatically adjusts in real-time — no manual intervention required.

1

Detect

Sensors on each hood monitor real-time temperature changes at every cooking station. Ambient temperature sensors track overall kitchen conditions.

2

Analyze

The AI algorithm compares feedback values from every sensor pair via core algorithm, calculating the optimal airflow requirements.

3

Execute

The control system sends commands to adjust damper angles and fan speeds — precisely regulating indoor temperature and air quality.

4

Optimize

During idle periods, the system automatically switches to micro-ventilation mode, maintaining slight negative pressure while minimizing energy use.

Industrial-Grade Sensor Design

Temperature sensors are mounted with metal sleeves for maximum protection and extended service life. Each sensor is equipped with a separate error-correction chip, ensuring long-term stable and high-accuracy operation — even in the harshest kitchen environments.

Problems & Solutions

What Pain Points Do We Solve?

Commercial kitchens face serious challenges. We've engineered precise solutions for every one.

Amazingly High Electricity Costs

Problem: Traditional exhaust fans run at full speed 100% of the time, wasting massive electricity.
✅ Solution: As per CVC testing report, our system achieves a total saving rate of 54.13%. Comprehensive saving in real projects is above 50%, with up to 80% in optimal conditions.

Grease & Smoke Escaping

Problem: Disgusting grease and smoke leaking from the exhaust system into the kitchen.
✅ Solution: Per-stove monitoring via sensor feedback ensures exhaust fumes are captured more accurately and effectively at the source.

Unbearable Duct & Electromagnetic Noise

Problem: Constant fan noise and duct resonance create an intolerable working environment for chefs.
✅ Solution: Advanced AI algorithm eliminates electromagnetic noise and air duct resonance, creating a calm kitchen atmosphere.

No Real-Time Monitoring

Problem: Infeasible to monitor and control the exhaust system in real-time.
✅ Solution: Real-time control, monitoring, and data tracing of fault history, power saving, and working states — all accessible remotely.

Unbalanced Air Pressure

Problem: When the exhaust system is on, doors get stuck due to unbalanced air pressure.
✅ Solution: Our system supports linkage control with fresh air fans for pressure compensation, preventing door suction issues.

High Maintenance Costs

Problem: Unsufferable heavy maintenance costs from belt wear and motor strain.
✅ Solution: Optimized soft-start curve reduces belt erosion and extends motor service life. Emergency backup power supply ensures continuous operation.
