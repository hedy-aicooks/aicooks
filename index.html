About DCKV

What is a Demand Control Kitchen Ventilation System?

A DCKV system modulates exhaust and supply fan speed based on real-time cooking activity detected by optical and current sensors — eliminating constant full-speed operation and significantly reducing energy, noise, and HVAC load.

System Definition

The AICOOKS DCKV (Demand Control Kitchen Ventilation) system utilizes intelligent AI algorithms and high-precision sensors to automatically adjust damper angles and fan speeds based on real-time sensor feedback — similar to how a variable-frequency air conditioner works, but engineered specifically for commercial kitchen exhaust systems.

Core Philosophy: "Let the kitchen breathe automatically — save energy, reduce noise, and create a comfortable cooking environment while meeting fire safety codes."

Why DCKV Matters

Traditional commercial kitchen exhaust fans run at full speed regardless of cooking activity, wasting massive amounts of electricity and over-ventilating the space. A DCKV system ensures fans operate only as needed, cutting energy consumption by 50%–80% while maintaining code-compliant airflow and improving kitchen comfort.
