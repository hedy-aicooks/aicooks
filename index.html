About DCKV

What is a Demand Control Kitchen Ventilation System?

A DCKV system modulates exhaust and supply fan speed based on real-time cooking activity detected by optical and current sensors — eliminating constant full-speed operation and significantly reducing energy, noise, and HVAC load.

System Definition

The AICOOKS DCKV (Demand Control Kitchen Ventilation) system utilizes intelligent AI algorithms and high-precision sensors to automatically adjust damper angles and fan speeds based on real-time sensor feedback — similar to how a variable-frequency air conditioner works, but engineered specifically for commercial kitchen exhaust systems.

Core Philosophy: "Let the kitchen breathe automatically — save energy, reduce noise, and create a comfortable cooking environment while meeting fire safety codes."

Why DCKV Matters

Traditional commercial kitchen exhaust fans run at full speed regardless of cooking activity, wasting massive amounts of electricity and over-ventilating the space. A DCKV system ensures fans operate only as needed, cutting energy consumption by 50%–80% while maintaining code-compliant airflow and improving kitchen comfort.

AI-Powered Intelligence

Smart algorithms process sensor data in real-time to optimize airflow dynamically based on cooking load.

Up to 80% Energy Saving

Variable speed operation ensures fans consume power only when cooking activity demands it. Dramatically reduce electricity bills.

Noise Elimination

Advanced AI eliminates duct resonance and electromagnetic noise by avoiding constant high-speed operation.

Real-Time Monitoring

How It Works

Four-Step Intelligent Ventilation Cycle

The DCKV system continuously monitors, analyzes, and optimizes kitchen exhaust airflow in a closed-loop cycle.

01

Detect

Optical and thermal sensors above each hood detect cooking activity and smoke levels in real-time. No manual override needed.

02

Analyze

AI controller calculates required exhaust airflow based on cooking load, hood type, and zone configuration. Optimizes for minimum energy use.

03

Execute

VFD ramps fan speed up or down; modulating dampers synchronize to maintain proper capture velocity. Supply air adjusts automatically.

04

Optimize

Continuous closed-loop ensures minimum code airflow while maximizing energy savings. System learns and adapts to kitchen patterns over time.

Solutions

DCKV Solves Your Kitchen's Biggest Challenges

Traditional constant-volume kitchen exhaust wastes energy and creates discomfort. See how DCKV transforms each problem into a solution.

High Electricity Bills

Fans run at 100% speed even when no cooking is happening, wasting thousands in electricity monthly.

→

DCKV modulates fan speed to match cooking demand. Save 50%–80% on energy costs.

Kitchen Smoke & Heat

Inadequate capture velocity leads to smoke escaping into the kitchen, creating unsafe working conditions.

→

Sensors detect smoke instantly and ramp up exhaust. Maintains code-compliant capture velocity at all times.

Duct Resonance & Noise

Constant high-speed fan operation creates duct vibration and unbearable noise levels in the kitchen.

→

Variable speed avoids resonant frequencies. AI actively eliminates duct resonance for a quieter kitchen.

No Real-Time Monitoring

Without monitoring, kitchen managers have no visibility into fan performance, energy usage, or potential faults.

→

Built-in LCD display and optional BMS/IoT integration. Track energy, runtime, and alarms in real-time.

Air Pressure Imbalance

Exhaust fans running at full speed create negative pressure, pulling conditioned air out and increasing HVAC costs.

→

DCKV balances exhaust with supply air modulation. Reduces HVAC heating/cooling load by 20%–30%.

High Maintenance Costs

Constant full-speed operation wears out fan motors, belts, and bearings — leading to frequent breakdowns and costly repairs.

→

Reduced runtime at high speed extends equipment life. Lower maintenance frequency and longer motor lifespan.
